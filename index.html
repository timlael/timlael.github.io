Welcome to timlael.github.io

 • Posted by Tim M. Lael on May 18, 2017  • 

This is a collection of some of my academic work which was created throughout the course of my studies at The University of Missouri - St. Louis which concluded May 13, 2017 when I was awarded a Bachelor of Science - Computer Science.

Most of the programs are written in C unless requirements dictated another language be used. Early on, I chose C as my primary language because of the challenge of implementing project requirements which would have been much more simple using an object-oriented language. After all, I was there to learn, so why not make it at least a little challenging. Later, I was pleasantly surprised when the utility of C and its close relationship with *nix proved very useful in Operating Systems classes when implementing memory management, process scheduling, IO and other low level tasks.
